Components on should see before purchasing

  • Processor: Check for speed and brand
  • RAM: More RAM is better for multitasking
  • Storage: Decide based on your usage, and see if it's expandable
  • Display: Larger and high resolution displays give better viewing
  • Battery life: Higher mAh means longer usage; check for fast charging.
  • Camera: Look at megapixels and extra modes (night, zoom, wide-angle).
  • Build Quality: Durable and good materials are important.
  • Operating System: Updated Android or iOS is safer.
  • Network Support: If you want 5G, check compatibility.
  • Price & Brand: Choose trusted brands and suitable budget.
